Step-by-Step: How to Speak with a Representative

  1. Dial (+1-855-550-0971) or 1-800-221-1212.
  2. Follow the automated prompts for your issue (e.g., “existing reservations”).
  3. To skip to a real person, press “0” or say “agent” or “representative”.
